An overhead lifeline system provides a secure, hands-free way for workers to move at height while maintaining a constant connection to a fall protection system. It typically consists of a rigid or flexible cable suspended overhead, along with connection points and fall arrest devices.

  1. Any working surface 4ft. off the ground must be equipped with a fall protection system. Specific industries must be fitted with the Overhead Lifeline System to allow workers at loading & unloading bays to prevent accidents in the event of a fall. The system arrests the fall and the person will be either lowered or rescued accordingly.
  2. Heinrich's Overhead Lifeline System provides a versatile yet economical fall prevention solution to the most challenging fall hazards. The fall-resistant system is engineered to meet the specific needs of the Transport, Industrial, Entertainment, Oil and Gas, Manufacturing, Warehouse, etc. industry.
  3. It enables workers to safely span long distances without needing to stop and re-attach to a new anchor point. It enables safe work at heights to be carried out on trucks, trains, aircraft, crane walkways and loading bays. It solves access and safety problems across various sectors and enhanced productivity at the site.
