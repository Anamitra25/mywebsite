At HSSPL, we use high-grade stainless steel 304 and 316 fasteners across our lifeline safety systems to ensure maximum durability, strength, and corrosion resistance in all working environments.

Our SS 304 fasteners are ideal for general industrial use, offering excellent resistance to rust and oxidation.

For harsher or coastal environments, we rely on SS 316, which contains molybdenum for superior resistance to chlorides, chemicals, and marine conditions.
