End Anchor

It can be used as an End Anchor for Roof structures like corrogated Sheet.This solution enables an easier management of fast mounting of the lifelines. All the while being more durable and resistant.

Also it is essential for securing the cable, transferring fall forces to the structure, maintaining tension, and ensuring the overall safety and stability.

  • Standard: EN 795 : 2012
  • Material: AISI 304 / 304L / 316
  • Allowable Working Load: 12 kN
  • Breaking Strength: 25 kN
  • Component Code: HHLC-02
