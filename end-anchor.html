End Anchor

It can be used as an End Anchor for Roof structures like corrogated Sheet.
This solution enables an easier management of fast mounting of the lifelines. All the while being more durable and resistant.
Standard: EN 795 : 2012
Material: AISI 304 / 304L / 316
Allowable working load: 12 kN.
Breaking Strength: 25 kN.
Component Code: HHLC-02
