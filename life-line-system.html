Life Line System

Working at height presents significant risks. Safety lifelines create a secure connection between the worker and an anchorage point, helping prevent falls and ensuring a safer working environment. Widely used in industries such as construction, oil and gas, telecommunications, shipbuilding, and warehousing, lifeline systems are essential for regulatory compliance and for safeguarding workers operating in elevated or hazardous areas.
